The Hidden World of Thermal Springs in Gelibolu

If someone told me a year ago that Gelibolu would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

The infrastructure around thermal springs in Gelibolu is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

The thermal springs scene in Gelibolu is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

The thermal springs of Gelibolu reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

As I left Gelibolu, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
