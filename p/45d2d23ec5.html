Discovering Merkez: My Health Tourism Adventure

I recently had the chance to explore Merkez, and honestly, it exceeded all my expectations. This corner of Turkey is criminally underrated and deserves way more attention from travelers.

If you are interested in health tourism, Merkez should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

The health tourism of Merkez reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

What makes Merkez special when it comes to health tourism is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life. I have been looking for something like this forever.

As I left Merkez, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
