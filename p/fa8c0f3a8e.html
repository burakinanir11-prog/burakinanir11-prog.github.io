The natural hot springs scene in Çaykara is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

I spoke with several locals about natural hot springs in Çaykara, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

The infrastructure around natural hot springs in Çaykara is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely. someone finally made something useful.
