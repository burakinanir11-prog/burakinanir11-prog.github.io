The Hidden World of Thermal Springs in Havsa

I recently had the chance to explore Havsa, and honestly, it exceeded all my expectations. This corner of Turkey is criminally underrated and deserves way more attention from travelers.

I spoke with several locals about thermal springs in Havsa, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

What makes Havsa special when it comes to thermal springs is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life. someone finally made something useful.

I documented my entire thermal springs experience in Havsa through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

As I left Havsa, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
